An interactive tool to find weather information directly from the station located at Southern Adventist University campus.

Sections

  • Weather Info: Displays summarized information about temperature, rain, relative humidity, and soil moisture for a selected range of dates.
  • Yearly Comparisons: Compare yearly temperature and precipitation against thirty year average.
  • Download Data: Allows to select a range of dates and download all variables available from our weather station.

About Soil Moisture

The Davis Instruments sensor measures on a scale of 0 (fully wet) to 200 (fully dry) centibars. The sensor measures the vacuum created in the soil by the lack of moisture.
